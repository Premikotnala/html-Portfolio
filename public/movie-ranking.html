The Best Movies According To Premi

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Happy Days

My favourite Telugu-language musical coming of age film .It tells the story of eight friends who become good friends in their freshman years at an engineering college.

vivah

My all time favorite hindi romantic film.It tells the story of two people,Shahid Kapoor(Sunil) and Amrita Rao(Prem), and their journey from engagement to marriage

Hridayam

It is One of my favourite Malayalam-language movie. It is a Indian romantic drama film that follows the story of Arun, a young man who enrolls in an engineering college and falls into bad habits. The film is about Arun's carefree bachelor days and how he matures through different stages of life.
